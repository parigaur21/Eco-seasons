Spring: Season of Rebirth

"The earth laughs in flowers." - Ralph Waldo Emerson

Green Tips for Spring

  • Plant Native Species: Choose plants native to your area. They require less water and support local pollinators like bees and butterflies.
  • Spring Cleaning: Use eco-friendly cleaning products like vinegar and baking soda instead of harsh chemicals.
  • Water Wisely: Collect rainwater for your garden using a rain barrel.
  • Get Moving: As the weather warms up, swap your car for a bike or walking for short trips.

Did You Know?

A single tree can absorb up to 48 pounds of carbon dioxide per year. Imagine the impact of planting a garden this spring!
