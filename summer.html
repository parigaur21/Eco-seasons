Summer: Sun & Sustainability

Enjoy the sunshine while keeping the planet cool.

Summer Sustainability Hacks

  • Natural Drying: Skip the dryer and hang your clothes outside to dry in the summer breeze.
  • Smart Cooling: Use blinds during the day to keep heat out and rely on fans instead of AC whenever possible.
  • Eco Sunscreen: Choose reef-safe sunscreen to protect our oceans while swimming.
  • Reusable Bottles: Stay hydrated with a reusable water bottle to reduce single-use plastic waste.

Did You Know?

Turning your thermostat up by just 2 degrees in summer can save over 5% on air conditioning costs and significantly reduce energy usage.
