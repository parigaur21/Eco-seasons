Autumn: Harvesting Change

Prepare for the cold while embracing the beauty of change.

Fall Eco-Actions

  • Compost Leaves: Don't bag your leaves! Mulch them or start a compost pile to create rich soil for next year.
  • Sustainable Harvest: Buy local, seasonal produce like pumpkins and apples to support local farmers and reduce transport emissions.
  • Draft Proofing: Check windows and doors for leaks. Sealing drafts now saves huge amounts of energy in winter.
  • Cozy Efficiency: Use heavy curtains to keep heat in during the night and open them during the day for natural solar heat.

Did You Know?

Composting organic waste can reduce household waste by up to 30%, keeping it out of landfills where it would generate methane gas.
